## Model accuracy & Edge Scanner (honest baseline)

**Edge Scanner ≠ arbitrage.** It ranks **model edge** (forecast vs line), not locked profit.

| Term | Meaning | Built? |
|------|---------|--------|
| Model edge | P(side) vs book implied | Yes (Edge Scanner) |
| Line shopping | Best over/under line across books | Partial (`line_comparison`) |
| True arb | Total implied prob < 1 across legs | No |
| Middle | Over low line + under high line | Detectable via `line_gap`; not arb |

**Distributions:** Engineering solid; **validated market edge weak.** Only rebounds→poisson
showed positive sweep ROI (+8.8%, n=482, **not significant**). Points/assists/pra stayed normal
(sweep inconclusive / negative ROI when bets placed). `--use-market-lines` needs more
`betting_lines` volume. High win rates in old real-data benchmarks used rolling mean as line
(model vs itself) — not book CLV.

**Edge Scanner vs full model (auto-bet gap):**

| | Scanner today | Auto-bet should use |
|--|---------------|---------------------|
| μ | Last-N mean | Rolling + defense + minutes + team priors |
| Dist | Always normal | `get_default_distribution(stat)` |
| σ | Sample std | Adjusted σ |
| Proof | None | `predictions` + CLV + backtest |

**Reuse:** `edge_scanner.py`, `line_comparison.py`, `compute_market_consensus`,
`clv_proxy.py`, hourly `_persist_predictions`.

## Known limitations

- Sparse scraped lines → Edge Scanner / Line Movement / CLV empty until Mac ETL runs.
- `betting_lines` thin → market-line validation blocked (backfill tooling exists).
- Team charts: lobby `points` only. `players.team` sparse (fallback: `matchup`).
- Scraping host = dev Mac; dev box may be Windows (`DataLoader` sqlite leak on temp DB teardown).
- **Do not use Edge Scanner for real-money bets** until WS9 + WS10 Phase 1 complete.
